about me

I am currently pursuing my Master's in Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University. Previously, I obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Neuroscience at Wellesley College, and a second Bachelor's in Integrated Computer Science from the University of British Columbia.

My multidisciplinary background makes me naturally inquisitive of the interaction between human behavior and technology. How can our understanding of human cognition drive design? What new technological mediums can arise from this knowledge? These are only some of the questions that I hope to get a chance to explore in the future.
